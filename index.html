SAUTI SOL MUSIC BAND

Brief History About Sauti Sol Band Music

The band, Sauti Sol, an assemblage of four amazingly talented artists,
has undoubtedly stuck on people's minds. They never disappoint by giving fans
what they yearn for — hit after hit of contemporary music composed and sang
with great vigour

Bien-Aimé Baraza

(talented songwriter, guitarist)

Bien-Anime (which means beloved in French) is the band’s
songwriter, vocalist as well as a guitarist. He was born on
December 28, 1987, making him 33 years old
as of 2020. Bien is from the Abaluhyia tribe
that hails from western Kenya.
Bien was a student at the United States International University
where he studied Journalism and
Media Studies. He graduated in 2011.

Willis Austin Chimano

(vocalist, keytar, and saxophone player)

Willis’ musical role in the band is noticeable as he sings in a melodious baritone voice. The Sauti Sol member is a vocalist, keytar, and saxophone player. Because of his flamboyant style, the 31-year-old has stood out in the band. Chimano was born on July 9, 1987. He graduated with a degree in Journalism & Media Studies from the University of Nairobi. After his degree, he chose to put his effort into the Sauti Sol music group that has grown to be a phenomenal success.

Savara Mudigi

(producer, drummer, vocalist, DJ, and actor)

Savara is the other immensely talented vocalist that captures the attention of the audience whenever he sings. He is a producer, drummer, vocalist, DJ, and actor. This incredible versatility
has been a great addition to the Sauti Sol band. The vocalist
was born on May 25, 1987 and raised in Kenya. He even studied
Commerce and Finance at the Africa Nazarene University. His girlfriend is
Endo. The two love birds met at a concert in Nairobi

Polycarp Otieno

(talented guitarist)

Polycarp, aka Fancy Fingers, is perhaps the
popular in the Sauti Sol music group as he doesn't sing.
And you know how tricky it is to stand out when
you are rarely part of the vocals that fans always
forward to. Nonetheless, the talented guitarist
managed to stay afloat in the band since its inception,
to the incredible bond the quartet shares.
